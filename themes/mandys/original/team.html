Meet the Experts

All members of our team are both style and colour experts. This means you don't need to break the creative process up by constantly explaining your look to different stylists. One of our stylists we'll take you from consultation to cut to style and to colour in one session.

Mandy, Head Stylist

As a Creative Director, it's my job to listen to what you want and show you what we can do. And when it comes to styling I'm a good all-rounder but my speciality is colours and the opportunity it gives me to create new positive looks for people.

Sihle, Stylist

I have been a stylist at Mandy's for more than 5 years. I'm a great all-round hairdresser, however my speciality lies within the afro hair category. I have been a finalist at the Black Like Me Hair Awards and count a number of photo shoots and magazine work under my belt.

Thembisa, Stylist

There's real creativity here. Like my colleagues, I'm most happy when you are happy and the way I achieve that is by trying to grasp what look exactly you're after. I know then that you'll leave feeling like a million Rands and my job is done.

Unathi, Stylist

I am a very creative individual, so the chance to work with like-minded people is fantastic. My speciality is colours and cuts and people are always amazed at how a subtle combination cannot only transform your hair but enhance the shape of your face.
